About us

Princeton has been a leading supplier of mechanical, electrical, instrumentation & safety products and services to different industries all over the world, for over two decades. Our commitment to excellence along with our excellent customer support has been driving us. We specialise in delivering high-quality products at an affordable price. Our team of experts will guide in choosing the most updated solutions which can be tailored according to your requirements.

We provide the following services to clients in Kuwait, UAE and all across the World.

Import and Export

At Princeton, we receive and process various shipments every month from both local and international market, including LCL, shipments, and full container.

Procurement

We take pride in out distribution network, highly trustworthy in the region. Lonestar delivers multiple orders in a day across the Kuwait and the world.

Mechanical

We provide the highest quality mechanical products like Pipes (Seamless and Welded pipes), Flanges, Fittings (Elbows & Tees), Fasteners, Valves, Gaskets & Packing and the many more to any part of the globe.

Electrical

We are the suppliers for a wide range of electrical products like Cables & Wires, Explosion proof light fittings, Conduit Pipes & Fittings, Cable Glands, Earthing & Lightning Protection, Cable Pulling Accessories, Cadweld etc.

Safety belt, body Harness, lanyard. First aid box, Eyewash station, shower station, Safety flashlight, Breathing apparatus, Ladders, Scaffolding Towers, Windsock & lockouts. Multi-gas, Personal Gas detectors, Gas alarm etc.
